EVENT INFORMATION

Tournament Format

Qualifying rounds (12-13 Feb) followed by Main Draw (14-15 Feb). Single elimination format with best-of-5 games until quarterfinals, then best-of-7.

Venue

Amundsen-Scott Arena, South Pole Station. Specially designed indoor facility maintaining optimal playing conditions in extreme environment.

Points & Prizes

Winner receives 1500 WTT ranking points and USD $75,000. Total prize money: USD $500,000 distributed among all events.

Antarctic Conditions

Special indoor climate control maintains standard table tennis conditions. All players provided with appropriate cold-weather gear for outside venue.

WTT Champion South Pole

The first World Table Tennis championship event held at the South Pole, celebrating the global reach of table tennis.
